# Investopoly Board - Clockwise Movement Order

Players start at position 0 (START) and move clockwise around the board. Here is the complete order of spaces:

## Bottom Row (Positions 0-8) - LEFT to RIGHT
- **Position 0**: START (Corner)
- **Position 1**: US Treasury Bond (Asset - Bond)
- **Position 2**: Market Event (Chance)
- **Position 3**: Corporate Bond (Asset - Bond)
- **Position 4**: S&P 500 ETF (Asset - ETF)
- **Position 5**: Capital Gains Tax (Tax)
- **Position 6**: Tech Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 7**: Blue Chip Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 8**: Dividend Stock (Asset - Stock)

## Right Side (Positions 9-17) - BOTTOM to TOP
- **Position 9**: MARKET EVENT (Corner)
- **Position 10**: Bitcoin (Asset - Crypto)
- **Position 11**: Real Estate ETF (Asset - ETF)
- **Position 12**: Market Event (Chance)
- **Position 13**: Ethereum (Asset - Crypto)
- **Position 14**: Growth Mutual Fund (Asset - Mutual Fund)
- **Position 15**: Transaction Fee (Tax)
- **Position 16**: Energy Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 17**: Altcoin Portfolio (Asset - Crypto)

## Top Row (Positions 18-26) - RIGHT to LEFT
- **Position 18**: DIVIDEND DAY (Corner)
- **Position 19**: Healthcare Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 20**: Market Event (Chance)
- **Position 21**: Inverse ETF (Asset - ETF) *Moves opposite to market*
- **Position 22**: Municipal Bond (Asset - Bond)
- **Position 23**: Income Tax (Tax)
- **Position 24**: Value Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 25**: Small Cap Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 26**: Balanced Fund (Asset - Mutual Fund)

## Left Side (Positions 27-35) - TOP to BOTTOM
- **Position 27**: MARKET EVENT (Corner)
- **Position 28**: Gold ETF (Asset - ETF)
- **Position 29**: Consumer Stock (Asset - Stock)
- **Position 30**: Market Event (Chance)
- **Position 31**: Gold (Asset - Commodity)
- **Position 32**: Index Mutual Fund (Asset - Mutual Fund)
- **Position 33**: Wealth Tax (Tax)
- **Position 34**: Emerging Market ETF (Asset - ETF)
- **Position 35**: Inflation-Protected Bond (Asset - Bond)

## Back to START
After position 35, the next move returns to position 0 (START), and the player receives $1000.
